Our Story...

After many years of dedicated service to the Galactic Empire, your captain, Grand Admiral Thrawn, decided it was time to retire. Unwilling to let go of the captain's chair, he decided to start a tour company in his favorite city: Chicago!

He has been sailing the waterways of the Windy City for over 3 years, and while it's no Star Destroyer, he's pretty stoked about the whole thing.
